About Me

Hello! My name is Courtney Cohn, and I am a sophomore journalism and government and politics double major at the University of Maryland, College Park. I have always loved writing, and I am looking to puruse my passion with a career in journalism. I am currently an opinion columnist for The Diamondback and a Features Editor for Mitzpeh. Last semester, I worked as a staff writer for The Campus Trainer, an online magazine. These diverse positions have given me valuable experience and have helped me improve my writing and editing skills. As a journalist, I strive to make a difference in my community and improve as many people's lives as I can.
